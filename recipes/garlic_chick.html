Garlic Chicken

to get back to the main page, Home

This garlic chicken recipe is a flavorful and easy-to-make dish that is perfect for any occasion. The combination of garlic, herbs, and spices creates a deliciously savory flavor that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Herbs
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Season the chicken with garlic, herbs, and spices.
  3. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
