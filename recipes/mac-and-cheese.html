This is a creamy and cheesy mac and cheese that is perfect for a comforting meal.

Mac and Cheese Ingredients

For this recipe you will need these ingredients to make a wonderful homemade super cheesy mac and cheese. this will defiently pair with some fried food or chicken. this will for sure be a crowd pleaser and will have people coming back for more.

  • Macaroni: This homemade mac and cheese starts with a box of uncooked macaroni.
  • Butter and Flour: These are used to make the roux, which thickens the cheese sauce.
  • Milk: This is added to the roux to create a creamy base for the cheese sauce.
  • Cheese: A combination of cheddar and mozzarella cheese is recommended for the best flavor and texture.
  • Seasonings: Salt, pepper, and optional spices like paprika or mustard powder can enhance the flavor.
  • Bread crumbs: Use store-bought dried bread crumbs for topping.

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles, drain, and transfer to a large mixing bowl.
  2. Make the cheese sauce, pour the sauce over the noodles, and stir to combine.
  3. Make the topping, spread it over mac and cheese, and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake in the oven until the top is golden brown and crispy.
