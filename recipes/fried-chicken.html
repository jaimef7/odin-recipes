This is a delicious fried chicken that has a crispy coating and is juicy on the inside.

Fried Chicken Ingredients

This recipe is recommened when you are having a gathering or a special occasion. that is outside on the weekend when yall want something that is easy to prepare and will please a crowd. This recipe will for sure make people come back for seconds.

  • For the seasoned flour mixture:All purpose flour with garlic salt, black pepper, paprika, poultry seasoning, and salt
  • For the beer batter:Beer, all purpose flour, egg yolks, salt, and black pepper
  • For the chicken: 1 whole chicken cut into pieces

Steps

  1. Make the seasoned flour mixture by combining all the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Prepare the beer batter by whisking together the beer, flour, egg yolks, salt, and black pepper in a separate bowl.
  3. Moisten the chicken in water and then dredge it in the seasoned flour mixture.
  4. Dip the chicken in the beer batter and then coat it with the seasoned flour mixture again.
  5. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C) and fry the chicken pieces until golden brown and cooked through.
