This lasagna is made with layers of pasta, cheese, and a rich meat sauce. with layers with tomato sauce.

lasagna indgredients

The recipe for this is recommened but the good thing about This lasagna is that it's very customizable and anyhting can be changed out to your liking. but for this versions of the reecipe here are the ingrediants.

  • Meat: This lasagna is going to use italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic:An Onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor to them
  • Tomato Products: you'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar:Two tablespoons of white sugar adds a subtle sweetness and echance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • lasagna noodles: use store bough noodles or home-made noodles.
  • Cheeses:Parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra fluffy.
  • Egg: an egg helps binds the ricotta so it does not ooze out of the lasagna and stays in when you cut it.

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

how to layer the lasagna

  1. Meat sauce.
  2. Noodles.
  3. Ricotta mixture.
  4. Mozzarella slices.
  5. Meat sauce.
  6. Parmesan cheese.
  7. repeat the layers, then top with remaining Parmesan.
