Slow Pork Sandwich

to get back to the main page, Home

This pulled pork recipe makes tender, juicy, and incredibly flavorful shredded pork. Barbecue-restaurant-worthy yet easier than you might expect, this slow-cooker recipe is simple enough for weeknights, game day, and perfect for feeding a crowd.

  • oil
  • pork
  • sauces
  • broth
  • seasonings

Instructions

  1. Preheat the slow cooker.
  2. Add the pork to the slow cooker.
  3. Add the sauces and broth.
  4. Cook on low for 8 hours.
  5. Shred the pork with a fork.
